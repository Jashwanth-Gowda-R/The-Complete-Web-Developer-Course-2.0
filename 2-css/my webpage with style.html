Shani

God of Deeds and Karma, Dispenser of Justice, King of Planets, Saturn

Shani is the child of Surya (Sun god) and Chaya (son of Sun god). He is additionally the sibling of Lord Yama (divine force of death). As a fervent lover of Lord Shiva, Shani was given the expert to remunerate or rebuff individuals for their great and awful deeds
